Pacencia Gayaman Gauken Golocan

A mother of 7, a grandmother of 21, and a great-grandmother of 27

Here's a timeline of Pacencia's life:

  • 1937 - Born in San Gabriel, La Union
  • 1958 - Married to Pedro A. Golocan Sr.
  • 1959 - Became a mother at the age of 20 years old.
  • 1989 - Went to the United States due to an immigration petition filed by her eldest daughter.
  • 1990 - Started working odd jobs to help her eldest daughter financially.
  • 1992 - Repatriated in the Philippines to help her husband Pedro in farming.
  • 1996 - Julius, her youngest son, died from a gunshot wound; she took care of her granddaughter, Noemie.
  • 1998 - Stayed in Baguio City to take care of her 12th grandson, Duane.
  • 2001 - Together with her husband, bought an expansion of their farmland in Isabela.
  • 2006 - Passed away at the age of 69 due to heart complications.
